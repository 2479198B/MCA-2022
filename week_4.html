jSymbolic Analysis

The following features were selected for the jSymbolic analysis:

  • Number of pitches: 43
  • Number of pitch classes: 11
  • Range: 47
  • Strong tonal centres: 1
  • Mean pitch: 64.38
  • Mean pitch class: 5.811
  • Most common pitch: 71
  • Most common pitch class: 4
  • Interval between most prevalent pitches: 7
  • Pitch variability: 11.09
  • Most common melodic interval: 2
  • Stepwise motion: 0.5487
  • Melodic thirds: 0.1222
  • Vertical unisons: 0.01808
  • Parallel fifths: 0
  • Parallel octaves: 0.001034

In addition to the recommended features, I selected features that had to do with voicing and melody since Bach's Air follows very traditional rules of harmony. I wanted to see where Bach diverged from these rules, and why. I selected vertical unisons, parallel fifths and parallel octaves for this reason. While jSymolic can find the above features in a piece, it does not display them and I did not find the parallel octaves with a brief look through the score. Since the piece is in a major key, I also found it relevant to include an analysis for melodic thirds.

Piano Roll and Pitch Histogram
